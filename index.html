Trust & security

Your data stays yours.

We work with sensitive commercial data, so we’re explicit about how we protect it — and about what “verified” actually means in every output we hand you.

🔒

You own your data

You keep your source and your licence. We never resell, share or repackage your data — it is used only to build and run your engagement, and nothing else.

◎

Confidential by default

Every engagement can be covered by an NDA before any data changes hands. Access is limited to the people working directly on your project.

✓

What “verified” means

Every figure traces back to the data you provided. Where the data is incomplete, we flag it rather than fill the gap — no fabricated numbers, no invented benchmarks, ever.

Our data-protection commitments

✓ You retain full ownership of all data you provide.
✓ An NDA is signed before any data is shared.
✓ Your data is used only for your engagement — never resold or shared.
✓ Access is limited to the team working on your project (least-privilege).
✓ Data is returned or deleted at the end of the engagement on request.
✓ We work within your data-residency and handling requirements.
✓ No new third-party data subscription is required.
✓ We complete your security questionnaire / DPA on request.

We don’t claim certifications we don’t hold. Instead, we work to your organisation’s security and compliance requirements, sign your agreements, and complete your due-diligence process before any data changes hands.

Privacy: Veridiqa collects only the contact details you choose to send us and the market data you share for an engagement. We do not sell or share your information. To ask a question, request deletion, or sign an NDA before sharing data, email contact@veridiqa.com. See our full Privacy & Terms.
